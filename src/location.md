## Tram/bus tickets

Same ticket for trams and buses. Cost 0.53 EUR for single ticket.
Buy at any kiosk/tobacco shop, or install the app [Moj ZET](https://www.zet.hr/tickets-and-fares/moj-zet-app/8111) and pay by scanning QR codes in the tram/bus.
